My Experience
2010
6 Years old
XP & Wii mods
At this stage in my life, I had just been introduced to digital games. From the puzzle widget on Windows XP to 3D Brawlers on the Nintendo Wii, I wanted to play them all. With the help of my father and online guides, I modified my wii console to accept sd-cards formatted with the WBFS-manager, so that I could play any game ROM I wished, without needing the physical disc.

It was also at this stage I got heavily invested in technology and it's capabilities. I loved heavily customising my devices, including the Windows XP family pc with the tweak UI powertoy.
