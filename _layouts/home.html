At this stage of my life, I was introduced to the captivating world of digital games. From the puzzle widget on Windows XP, testing my ingenuity, to the exhilarating 3D Brawlers on the Nintendo Wii, I was drawn to explore every genre. With the assistance of my father and online resources, I successfully modified my Wii console to accommodate SD-cards formatted with the WBFS manager, granting me the freedom to indulge in any game ROM I desired, without the need of a physical copy.

During this period, I developed a profound interest in technology and its vast potential. I wholeheartedly delved into the realm of technology, mastering its intricacies and becoming a proficient user. My focus on customization proved paramount in satisfying my unique needs. An instance of this was my family's Windows XP PC, which I meticulously optimized and enhanced with powerful tools like the Tweak UI PowerToy.
